Nothing like the present

Today I study computer science at a great university with even greater schoolmates.
My appartement is a mere trainstation away where I live with my beautiful girlfriend whom I've been together with since 2013.
She makes me smile with everything she does and truly is my best friend. I could go on forever about her so it's probably best if I stop now.

I am particularly interested in efficient algorithms and datastructures, aswell as anything that has to do with databases or data warehousing.
The pure aspect of problem-solving is what most intrigues me about programming. Even more interesting is the fact that most problems that arise within programming
are errors in the programmers way of thinking, not the machine. Therefore I often practice logical reasoning and I enjoy playing sudoko daily.
Troubleshooting and testing has been a big part of my studies and I look forward to putting my skills to the test and improving in a working environment.
My vision for my code is for it to be readable, structured and well-documented, making it easily accessible for anyone who works on it as well as for future self.

Apart from such logical games I am also an avid gamer. It's a hobby that has occupied most of my freetime during life and that's nothing I intend to change anytime soon.
I consider myself a “curious gamer” as I’ve made a habit of playing most newly released titles, regardless of genre, and completing well over half of them.
Studying game development has truly changed the experience of gaming for me, making me analyze mechanics and assets as I play.
I feel that my diverse gaming experience helps me create more player-friendly features and assets.
